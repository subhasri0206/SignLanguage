Introduction

Welcome to our platform, designed to bridge communication gaps between the hearing and deaf communities. Using advanced technology, we facilitate interaction through speech-to-sign language conversion powered by a lifelike avatar.

We are committed to continuously improving and expanding our services. Our goal is to introduce full sign language recognition and vice versa, making communication more accessible and empowering for everyone. Stay tuned for future updates as we work to enhance our platform and foster a truly inclusive experience.

Explore our work at the Work section and see how we’re changing the way we communicate.

Note: Currently, only speech-to-text conversion is supported. Full sign language recognition and translation are coming soon!

About Us

We are a group of passionate and dedicated final-year students, committed to creating technology that has a meaningful social impact. As part of our final-year project, we wanted to develop something that could truly make a difference in the world, which led us to the idea of bridging communication barriers between the hearing and deaf communities.

Our project aims to provide speech-to-sign language and speech-to-text conversion, facilitated through an interactive avatar. This technology will allow for seamless communication between individuals who use spoken language and those who use sign language. By translating speech into sign language and vice versa, our platform opens up new opportunities for inclusive communication in everyday life.

We chose this project because of its immense potential for social good. We recognized the importance of inclusivity and accessibility in communication and wanted to create a solution that would empower people to interact freely without the limitations of language barriers. Our goal is to make the world more connected by providing an easy-to-use platform that ensures everyone can participate in conversations and engage meaningfully with others.

Each of us on the team brings a unique set of skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities that have been crucial in the development of this project. Through teamwork and collaboration, we have been able to bring this idea to life, and we’re excited to continue refining and expanding its features. As final-year students, this project has been a culmination of all the learning and experiences we’ve gathered throughout our academic journey, and we couldn’t be more proud of the work we’ve accomplished so far.
